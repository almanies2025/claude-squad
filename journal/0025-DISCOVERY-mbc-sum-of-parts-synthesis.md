# DISCOVERY: MBC SOTP and Final Synthesis Complete

**Date:** 2026-04-24

## Context

Continuation of the MBC Group valuation exercise. Three analysis documents were completed:

1. **DCF Model** (`02-dcf-model.md`): Base case SAR 48.8, sensitivity table across WACC 8–12% and terminal growth 3.5–5%
2. **Comparable Companies** (`03-comparable-companies.md`): Range SAR 16–59, central estimate SAR 35–50
3. **Sum-of-Parts** (`04-sum-of-parts.md`): NEW — segment-level valuation
4. **Final Synthesis** (`05-final-synthesis.md`): NEW — investment rating and recommendation

## Finding: SOTP Value Exceeds Market Price by 86%

MBC's three segments valued separately:

| Segment              | Base Case Equity (SAR M) | Multiple Applied |
| -------------------- | ------------------------ | ---------------- |
| BOCA (Broadcast)     | 8,057                    | 10.5x EBITDA     |
| Shahid (Streaming)   | 7,967                    | 5x EV/Revenue    |
| M&E (Media & Events) | 417                      | 10x EBITDA       |
| **Total**            | **16,441**               |                  |
| Market Cap           | 8,844                    |                  |
| **Implied Premium**  | **+86%**                 |                  |

Key insight: BOCA alone (SAR 8,057M) nearly equals MBC's entire market cap (SAR 8,844M). Shahid and M&E are essentially free at current prices.

## Finding: DCF and SOTP Converge at SAR 48–50

Two completely independent methods arrived at nearly identical values:

- **DCF base case:** SAR 48.8 (WACC 10%, g 4.5%)
- **SOTP base case:** SAR 49.4 (BOCA 10.5x, Shahid 5x, M&E 10x)

This convergence is significant. DCF projects future cash flows; SOTP benchmarks against comparable businesses today. When both point to the same range, confidence in the estimate increases — but both share a common assumption about Shahid's growth trajectory, so they may be wrong in the same direction.

## Finding: Market Assigns Near-Zero Value to Shahid

At market price (SAR 26.26):

- BOCA contributes SAR 8,057M equity → implies BOCA is 91% of market cap
- Shahid + M&E contribute SAR 8,384M in SOTP → but market attributes 0 of this

The market prices MBC as a mature broadcast business with a "free" streaming option. This is either a massive underpricing of Shahid's optionality — or a rational discount for streaming execution risk in MENA.

## Key Catalysts for Re-Rating

1. FY2026 EBITDA margin hits 12%+ — confirms streaming scale economics
2. International analyst coverage initiates — raises visibility
3. Shahid hits 5M+ subscribers — triggers streaming multiple re-rating
4. Strategic transaction — precedent (Paramount 7.5x) suggests strategic buyers would pay 40–60% premium
