## Discovery: mBridge Is Real Operational Infrastructure

**What was found**: mBridge (BIS Innovation Hub + China + Hong Kong + UAE + Saudi Arabia + Thailand) crossed $55 billion in cumulative cross-border CBDC transactions and 4,000+ transactions as of January 2026. MVP reached mid-2024. This is a **2,500x increase since 2022**.

**Why it matters**: The framing in the original brief — "mBridge as building" — undersells what's already happened. mBridge is not a prototype. It is settling real trade between sovereign CBDCs across jurisdictions, bypassing SWIFT and the dollar intermediate layer entirely.

**The nuance**: $55B is a rounding error vs. $100T+ in global FX markets. But the growth rate is what matters — exponential adoption in infrastructure projects follows S-curves, and mBridge appears to be in the early exponential phase.

**What this changes**: The question is no longer "will alternative payment infrastructure emerge?" It is "which corridors will mBridge capture next, and how fast will it scale?"

**Sources**: BIS official statement (Nov 2024), Reuters (Jan 2026), The Block, Cointelegraph, TradingView
