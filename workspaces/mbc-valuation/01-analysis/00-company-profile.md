## Key Observations

1. **Revenue CAGR 2020–2025: ~18.4%** — strong top-line growth driven by Shahid streaming
2. **Path to profitability**: FY2020–2023 losses, first sustained profitability in FY2024
3. **Negative FCF in FY2025**: Working capital normalization after rapid growth; operations still cash-generative
4. **Minimal leverage**: Net cash position of ~SAR 1.2B; Total debt/equity only 3%
5. **Low float**: Listed Jan 2024 — only ~2 years of public trading history
6. **Shahid growth**: 34.1% YoY revenue growth in 4Q 2025 — fastest-growing segment
7. **Inventory anomaly**: Inventory halved from SAR 2.7B to SAR 1.8B in FY2025 (likely content production cycle)
