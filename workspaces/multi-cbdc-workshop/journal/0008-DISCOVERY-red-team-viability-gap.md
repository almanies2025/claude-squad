# DISCOVERY: No Credible First-Customer Path Exists

**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Phase**: 04-validate (red team)

## Finding

The workshop's first-customer strategy has a critical gap: there is no viable path from "unknown provider" to "first cohort" without existing central bank credentials.

**Why**: Central bank procurement requires:

1. Vendor pre-qualification (can take 6-18 months)
2. Reference clients from similar institutions
3. Existing relationships with relevant departments
4. Often: a formal tender regardless of value threshold

**How to apply**: The workshop cannot launch without resolving the first-buyer problem. The HKMA co-sponsorship angle is the only credible entry point, but HKMA will not co-sponsor without a demonstrated offering and references.
