# RISK: Zero-Track-Record Provider Cannot Command $8K/day

**Date**: 2026-04-30
**Phase**: 04-validate (red team)

## Risk

An unknown provider with no central bank clients charging $8K/participant/day will face immediate price resistance. Central bank training budgets are real but procurement requires vendor vetting.

**Likelihood**: Critical (near-certain without mitigation)
**Impact**: Workshop cannot close first cohort at target price

## Mitigation

1. Partner with an established training broker (euromoney, centralbanking.com) who has existing vendor panel status
2. Offer first cohort at $3-4K/day as "founding participant" rate to establish track record
3. Use the FX Simulation's open-source credibility (available on GitHub) as proof of concept

**Why this matters**: The $8K/day figure is based on BIS-level market rates. A provider without credentials starts at 30-50% of that, not 100%.
