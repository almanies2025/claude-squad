## Finding

The CNY-dominance thesis requires HKMA as institutional proof-of-concept. No fallback central bank (MAS, BOT, CBUAE) provides the same anchor for the clearing-hub mechanism. If HKMA declines — which is the most likely outcome (40% non-response, 30% polite decline) — the workshop loses its differentiated institutional story and becomes a generic multi-CBDC seminar.

### Fallback Central Bank Assessment

| Central Bank           | CNY Dominance Anchor Fit                                                               | Verdict        |
| ---------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| MAS (Singapore)        | No CNY clearing relationship; FinTech credibility strong but wrong institutional story | Partial backup |
| BOT (Bank of Thailand) | Project Inthanon-Lionrock multi-CBDC work; small RMB user; wrong scale                 | Weak fallback  |
| CBUAE (UAE)            | Project Aber multi-CBDC; BIS member; geographically diversifying but wrong story       | Weak fallback  |
| Bank of England        | Major CBDC programme; BIS member; but UK is not CNY clearing hub                       | Wrong anchor   |

### The Structural Problem

The CNY-dominance thesis depends on:

1. PBOC RTGS infrastructure
2. Hong Kong as designated offshore CNY clearing hub
3. The institutional relationship between HKMA and PBOC

Without HKMA as the concrete instantiation of "clearing hub," the thesis becomes abstract. A generic multi-CBDC workshop can be sourced from BIS itself, academic institutions, or consulting firms. There is no differentiated value proposition for the specific Terrene Foundation workshop without the HKMA institutional anchor.

## Implication

**This is not a risk to be managed — it is a structural GAP in the strategy.** The strategy requires HKMA. If HKMA does not engage, the engagement path collapses. There is no fallback that preserves the CNY-dominance thesis as a workshop anchor.

## Recommended Actions

1. Before approaching HKMA, pre-qualify MAS as a parallel track (not fallback)
2. Reframe HKMA ask: technical advisor/observer, not co-sponsor — reduces political cost
3. Develop an alternative workshop narrative that does not depend on HKMA
4. Accept that the CNY-dominance thesis may be a workshop topic, not a sponsorship rationale
