## Core Idea

A SaaS product targeting US private equity firms that monitors KPIs across all portfolio companies in real time — replacing the manual quarterly spreadsheet process with automated anomaly detection and early warning signals.

## The Problem

A US PE firm owning 10–20 portfolio companies today:

- Receives data manually (Excel, email, PDF) from each company on a quarterly basis
- Has no visibility into what's happening between board meetings
- Discovers margin compression, churn spikes, or working capital deterioration only when it's too late to act
- Has no standardized view across the portfolio — every company reports differently

## The Product

Pull ERP/accounting data from portfolio companies automatically, normalize it across companies, and surface anomalies before the quarterly board pack lands.

## Key Features (Initial Thinking)

1. ERP/accounting integrations (QuickBooks, NetSuite, Sage, SAP)
2. Standardized KPI framework across portfolio companies
3. Anomaly detection — margin compression, churn, working capital deterioration
4. Early warning alerts to PE partners/operating partners
5. Automated LP reporting generation

## Target Customer

- US mid-market PE firms ($500M–$5B AUM)
- Owning 10–20 portfolio companies
- Currently using Excel + email for portfolio monitoring
