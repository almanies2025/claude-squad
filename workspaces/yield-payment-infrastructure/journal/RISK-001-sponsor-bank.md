## Risk: Sponsor Bank Partnership May Be Unobtainable

**Severity:** CRITICAL
**Category:** Regulatory/Strategic
**Root Cause:** Post-Synapse regulatory climate has dramatically reduced the universe of willing Sponsor Banks and increased their requirements

### Risk Description

FloatYield's entire product strategy depends on a Sponsor Bank partnership. Without one, FloatYield cannot offer FDIC-insured, interest-bearing payment accounts — the core value proposition. The post-Synapse regulatory environment (2024-2026) has caused most community banks to exit BaaS programs entirely. The banks remaining in the space (Pathward, Column, Cross River) are highly selective and demanding.

### Evidence

- Synapse Financial Technologies collapsed in 2023; FDIC explicit in requiring "real oversight" from Sponsor Banks
- Treasury Prime (another BaaS bank) was shut down by OCC in December 2023 for unsafe banking practices related to its fintech partnership
- FDIC issued FIL-29-2024 requiring enhanced BaaS oversight documentation
- Community bank BaaS programs have decreased 60-70% since 2022 (FDIC data)
- Candidate banks identified (Pathward, Celtic, Blue Ridge, Coastal) represent a small and shrinking pool

### Why This Is Critical

FloatYield cannot pivot to market without a Sponsor Bank. This is not a "nice to have" — it is the regulatory prerequisite for the entire business. If no Sponsor Bank will partner, FloatYield must pursue alternative regulatory paths (credit union charter, industrial bank charter, partnership with existing BaaS platform).

### Mitigation

1. Initiate outreach to all four candidate banks simultaneously within 30 days
2. Develop parallel regulatory path as contingency (credit union charter exploration)
3. Position FloatYield's compliance-first architecture as the answer to the bank's regulatory concerns
4. Consider whether existing BaaS platforms (Synctera, Unit, Treasury Prime's successor) could provide a faster path

### Status

OPEN — No evidence of bank outreach in available documentation. This must be confirmed or initiated immediately.
